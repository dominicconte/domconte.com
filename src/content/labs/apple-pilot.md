Apple Pilot is a personal trading-analysis dashboard. It pulls live market data through the Alpaca API and lays out portfolio snapshots, performance metrics, technical patterns and a watchlist in one clean view - with hooks in place for an AI signal-analysis and decision engine.

A work in progress, and mostly an excuse to play with real-time financial data, charting and autonomous decision-making outside the day job.

Built with React, Vite and Express, with a local SQLite store.
