Most people walk into a doctor's appointment and forget half of what they meant to say. MedicalBuddy fixes that. You write freely about what's worrying you, and it helps shape that into a calm, organised summary - symptoms, timeline, questions - that you can hand to your GP or export as a PDF.

It's deliberately warm rather than clinical, with clear safety disclaimers throughout: a tool to help you communicate, not a substitute for medical advice.

A prototype, built with Next.js and the Claude API.
