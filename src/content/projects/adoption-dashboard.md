## The problem

Firms invest in AI tools and then have no idea whether anyone uses them. "We rolled it out" is not the same as "it changed how people work", and without visibility, stalled adoption looks identical to success right up until renewal.

## The approach

A simple, privacy-respecting dashboard that tracks usage at the team level - not to surveil individuals, but to show leadership where adoption is real, where it's flat, and where a champion is needed.

## The outcome

The honest signal changed conversations. Instead of debating whether a tool was "good", teams could see where it had taken hold and intervene where it hadn't.

## What I learned

Measuring adoption is itself an intervention. The moment usage became visible, the questions got better - and so did the decisions.
