AI-native businesses are product led, fast, and scalable. Most law firms are not. This session showed how CMS built and funded new legal products in just three months through a structured accelerator for the AI age - the mindset shift, governance changes, and practical steps required to turn legal expertise into repeatable, revenue-generating products that the firm owns.

Three takeaways:

- **Recognise who your real competition is** - the product-led, AI-native challengers turning legal expertise into repeatable products - and why most firms' billable-hour model leaves them unable to respond at the same speed.
- **A practical model for turning expertise into products** - how CMS ran a firm-wide innovation accelerator that took 400+ ideas to funded products with working prototypes in about three months, including the mindset shift and governance changes that made it work.
- **Why owning your IP is the whole game** - how to build products your firm owns and can sell more than once, rather than feeding your expertise into a vendor's tool that gets sold to the firm down the road.
